Who are we?

Sheikh Broadband Internet is a Broadband Internet Service Provider for rural customers. Sheikh Broadband Internet separates itself from the rest of the internet providers by applying and receiving fully licensed spectrum from Industry Bangladesh.

This means our technology is fully endorsed by the federal government, allowing Sheikh Broadband Internet to offer fast and reliable internet to our users.

Broadband Internet Service

Most rural users think satellite is the only way to get internet. But Sheikh Broadband Internet uses earth-bound UHF band signals from company radio masts directly to your home with state-of-the-art aluminum yagi antennas.
